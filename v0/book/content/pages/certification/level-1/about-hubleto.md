# Lesson 1: About Hubleto

## What is Hubleto

Hubleto's headline says it is a `Business Application Hub`. At a first glance, it may be a CRM or ERP solution. But it is not only that.

Hubleto is a **platform** to build web-based applications to streamline business processes.

It is also an **ecosystem** of apps that you can build, share, reuse or offer to the community.

## Who is Hubleto for

Ideal user for Hubleto is a small and medium company with up to approximately 100 employees.

However, it can be anybody looking for a solution to digitalize and streamline its processes and collaboration space.

## Key features

  * Opensource and selfhosted
  * Large set of free-to-use community apps available
  * Clean and easy to understand software architecture
  * Modern and intuitive user interface
  * Strictly separated backend and frontend development.
